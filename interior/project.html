Featured Projects

At HASAN Interiors, we pride ourselves on creating spaces that resonate with personality, functionality, and style. Each project we undertake is a testament to our commitment to excellence and creativity. One of our standout achievements is a modern apartment makeover in the bustling city of Mumbai. The project involved transforming a 3BHK apartment into a sleek, contemporary living space. With open floor plans, custom furniture, and ambient lighting, the home now offers a seamless blend of comfort and sophistication. In Goa, we designed the interiors of a luxury beachfront villa, combining elegance with the beauty of natural materials. Expansive glass panels allow breathtaking sea views, while the interior features include grand chandeliers, spa-like bathrooms, and outdoor lounges that provide a perfect retreat for relaxation. For a tech startup in Bangalore, we created a modern workspace that fosters creativity and collaboration. The office design includes open desks, private meeting rooms, and breakout areas. To add vibrancy, biophilic elements like indoor plants and energy-efficient smart lighting systems were incorporated. Our expertise also extends to transforming heritage spaces, as demonstrated by our boutique hotel renovation in Jaipur.
